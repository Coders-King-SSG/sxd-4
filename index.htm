St. Xavier's School

Affiliated to the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examination, New Delhi

St. Xavier’s School Doranda, Ranchi, is named after St. Francis Xavier, a Jesuit saint of the 16th Century who travelled to India. It was founded in 1960 and is managed by the Fathers of the Society of Jesus popularly known as Jesuits. It is affiliated to the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examination, New Delhi. It prepares students for the ICSE (10th - only boys) and ISC (12th – Co-ed) examinations conducted by the Council. The School conducts classes for Plus Two in Science and Commerce streams. The management of the School is vested in the Ranchi Jesuit Society. The President of the Governing Body is Rev. Fr. Provincial of the Ranchi Jesuit Province

Achievements

01.
DOX Interschool Competition

The DOX Competitions is conducted by the Alumini of St. Xavier’s School and is an Academic competition consisting of Economics, Poetry Writing, History, Polity and Hindi among schools from Ranchi. Our School stood first🥇.

02.
Zonal Basket-Ball Championship

The Zonal Basket-Ball Championship was held amongst three districts; Ranchi, Hazaribagh and Bokaro. 25 schools participated in this zonal competition. We won the First Prize🥇.

03.
All-Ranchi Debate
Competition

The All-Ranchi Debate Competition was held amongst ALL schools of Ranchi. It was held in 3 levels; Class Level, School Level and final Zonal Level. This event was scheduled for two months. I won the first prize🥇! I have the pic in the below carousel. The topic was "Absolute Freedom".
